The Protocol

The Rules

Every rule must be completed
every single day. Miss one.
Restart from Day 1.
01
Physical

Two Workouts. Every Day.

Complete two separate workouts daily, each a minimum of 45 minutes. One workout must be performed outdoors — rain, cold, travel, and excuses are not valid reasons to skip. The two workouts must be at least four hours apart and cannot be combined or split into shorter sessions.

02
Mental

Daily Mental Training

Spend a minimum of 10 minutes every single day practicing a skill that actively challenges your mind — a language or a musical instrument. The same skill must be practiced consistently across all 90 days. Passive listening and consumption do not count. You must be actively learning and building.

03
Diet

Clean Diet. No Exceptions.

Follow a strict, clean diet for all 90 days. No alcohol. No smoking. No cheat meals of any kind. Drink a minimum of three litres of water daily. The goal is not aesthetic transformation — it is the daily discipline of choosing the harder, better option every single time it matters.

04
Recovery

One Rest Day Per Week

Take one full rest day per week with no structured workouts. Your diet remains completely strict. Your mental skill practice is still mandatory. Rest is not a reward or a break from the program — it is part of the protocol. Recovery is built into the system deliberately.

The Failure Rule

There are no partial credits. No grace periods. No 'I was sick' passes. No travel exemptions. No negotiations of any kind.

If you miss a single rule on any single day — you restart from Day 1. Full stop.

This rule is not a punishment. It is the entire point of the program. Without consequences, commitment is just intention. And intention without follow-through is worthless.

The restart rule is what makes every completed day mean something. It is the thing that separates alpha90 from every other challenge you've done and quit.

This is the rule that will test you the most. It is also the rule that will change you the most.
