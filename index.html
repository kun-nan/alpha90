The Core

The Rules

01
Physical

Two Workouts. Every Day.

Complete two separate 45-minute sessions daily. One MUST be outdoors in the elements — rain or shine — as a test of environment control.

02
Mental

Language Training

Spend at least 10 minutes every single day actively learning a new language. Consistency in mental progression is mandatory for cognitive discipline.

03
Diet

Clean Protocol. No Deviations.

Follow a strict, clean nutritional plan. No alcohol. No cheat meals. No smoking. 3 litres of water mandatory. No compromises.

04
Recovery

Deliberate Recuperation

Allow the body one rest day per week with zero structured physical training. Diet and mental rules still apply. Rest is a tactical requirement.

The Failure Rule

If you fail a single rule on any single day — you restart from Day 1 immediately. There are no excuses, no travel grace, and no negotiations.

This rule is the heartbeat of alpha90. Without a real consequence for failure, your word has no weight. Prove that yours does.

Clarity

Common Doubts

What counts as "clean diet"?
Whole foods. No processed sugar, alcohol, or nicotine. The specific plan is yours—the discipline of following it is the program.
What if I'm traveling?
The rules don't change. You find a park or a hotel gym. You eat clean in the lounge. Travel is a test, not an out.
What counts as Language Training?
Active learning via apps (Duolingo), tutors, or technical study. Passively listening to a podcast does not count as effort.
