The Protocol

The Rules

01
Physical

Two Workouts. Every Day.

Complete two separate 45-minute sessions daily. One MUST be outdoors — rain, cold, and travel are tests, not excuses.

02
Mental

Daily Mental Training

Spend 10 minutes practicing a complex skill like a language or musical instrument. Unbroken repetition builds new neuro-pathways.

03
Diet

Clean Diet. No Exceptions.

Follow a strict, clean diet. No alcohol. No smoking. No cheat meals. Drink 3 litres of water daily.

04
Recovery

One Rest Day Per Week

Recover once a week with no structured workouts. Diet and mental practice remain mandatory. Recovery is part of the architecture.

The Failure Rule

If you miss a single rule on any single day — you restart from Day 1. Full stop.

Without consequences, commitment is just intention. And intention without follow-through is worthless.

Friction Removal

Common Doubts

What counts as a "clean diet"?
Whole foods only. No processed sugars, no alcohol, no smoking, and zero cheat meals.
What if I travel internationally?
The rules do not change. Travel is the ultimate test of the protocol.
What counts as mental training?
Actively building a difficult skill. Musical instruments, languages, or deep technical learning.
Do I need to document it publicly?
Highly recommended for accountability. Tag @alpha90program.
