The Protocol

The Rules

Every rule must be completed
every single day. Miss one.
Restart from Day 1.
01
Physical

Two Workouts. Every Day.

Complete two separate 45-minute sessions daily. One MUST be outdoors — rain, cold, and travel are tests, not excuses. Sessions must be separated by at least 4 hours.

02
Mental

Daily Mental Training

Spend 10 minutes practicing a skill that challenges your mind — a language or musical instrument. Consistency is key; build a new neuro-pathway through unbroken repetition.

03
Diet

Clean Diet. No Exceptions.

Follow a strict, clean diet. No alcohol. No smoking. No cheat meals. Drink 3 litres of water daily. The goal is the discipline of choosing the harder, better option every time.

04
Recovery

One Rest Day Per Week

Allow the body to recover once a week with no structured workouts. Diet and mental skill practice remain mandatory. Recovery is a deliberate part of the protocol architecture.

The Failure Rule

There are no partial credits. No grace periods. No travel exemptions. No negotiations.

If you miss a single rule on any single day — you restart from Day 1. Full stop.

This rule is the entire point. Without consequences, commitment is just intention. And intention without follow-through is worthless.

It is the thing that separates alpha90 from every challenge you've done and quit.

Friction Removal

Common Doubts

What counts as a "clean diet"?
Whole foods only. No processed sugars, no alcohol, no smoking, and zero cheat meals. The specific plan is yours—the discipline of following it is the program.
Does swimming count as the outdoor workout?
Yes, if it's in an outdoor pool or open water. Indoor pools only count as your indoor workout. The outdoor session's purpose is exposure to the elements.
What if I travel internationally?
The rules do not change. You find a park, a sidewalk, or a road. You eat clean in the airport. Travel is the ultimate test of the protocol.
Can both workouts be outdoors?
Yes. One *must* be outdoors. You are free to do both in the elements if you choose.
What counts as mental training?
Actively building a difficult skill. Duolingo counts if engaged, but musical instruments, languages, or deep technical learning sessions are the gold standard.
Do I need to document it publicly?
It is not a requirement for finishing, but it is highly recommended for accountability. Tag @alpha90program to join the community.
Can I use supplements?
Yes, provided they align with your "clean diet" definition and do not contain banned substances (alcohol/nicotine).
Is there a specific workout plan?
No. You pick the movement. The program is about the discipline of showing up, not the specific set of exercises.
